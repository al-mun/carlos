About Carlos Tamayo

Carlos Tamayo was born in Cali, Colombia in 1988. He studied at the Departmental Institute of Fine Arts in the city of Cali. He is interested in studying and investigating the different mining processes in Colombia. Specifically, he uses his graphic work to give value to both the labor of the miner, as well as the damages suffered by the natural resources.
